# Business KPI Dashboard

## Overview
This project simulates a real-world business performance reporting 
workflow for a retail company. Using the Superstore Sales dataset, 
I designed an end-to-end KPI framework and interactive dashboard 
to support data-driven decision-making at the management level.

## Business Questions Answered
- Are we hitting our sales and revenue targets?
- Where are the biggest performance gaps across regions and categories?
- What trends should leadership act on next quarter?

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel (Pivot Tables, Power Query, Variance Analysis)
- Power BI (Interactive Dashboard)
- Dataset: Superstore Sales (Kaggle)

## Key KPIs Tracked
- Budget vs. Actual Sales (Monthly Variance)
- Profit Margin by Category
- Regional Revenue Performance
- Customer Segment Contribution
- Quarter-on-Quarter Growth Trend

## Key Findings
- West region consistently outperformed targets by 12%
- Technology products drove highest profit margins
- Q4 showed sales spike but margin drop — indicating heavy discounting
